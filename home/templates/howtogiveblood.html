How to Give Blood

Learn the step-by-step process of donating blood.

Step 1: Registration

When you click at the donor form, you’ll need to:

  • Provide valid information in the form.
  • Fill out a health history questionnaire.

Step 2: Health Check

A medical professional will:

  • Check your hemoglobin levels.
  • Measure your blood pressure and pulse.
  • Ask about your medical history.

Step 3: Donation

The donation process takes about 10-15 minutes:

  • You’ll be seated comfortably.
  • A sterile needle will be used to collect blood.
  • You’ll donate approximately one pint of blood.

Step 4: Recovery

After donation, you’ll:

  • Rest for 10-15 minutes.
  • Enjoy light refreshments.
  • Receive aftercare instructions.
