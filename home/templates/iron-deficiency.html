What is Iron Deficiency?

Iron deficiency occurs when your body doesn't have enough iron to produce hemoglobin, which is essential for carrying oxygen in your blood.

  • Symptoms: Fatigue, weakness, pale skin, and shortness of breath.
  • Causes: Poor diet, blood loss, or inability to absorb iron.
