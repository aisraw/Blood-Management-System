Blood Pressure Check

Check your blood pressure easily with our advanced testing methods.

Step 1: Preparation

Avoid caffeine, exercise, and smoking for at least 30 minutes before your test. Sit quietly for 5 minutes before measurement.

Step 2: Proper Positioning

Sit comfortably with your back supported and feet flat on the floor. Rest your arm on a table at heart level.

Step 3: Measurement Process

Our nurse will place the cuff on your bare upper arm and take multiple readings for accuracy.

Step 4: Get Your Results

Your blood pressure readings will be analyzed and explained to you immediately.
