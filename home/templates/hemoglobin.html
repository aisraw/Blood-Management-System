Hemoglobin Test

Check your hemoglobin levels to stay healthy and fit.

Category Hemoglobin Range (g/dL)
Men 13.8 - 17.2
Women 12.1 - 15.1
Children 11.0 - 16.0

Why is Hemoglobin Important?

  • It carries oxygen throughout your body.
  • Maintains energy levels and prevents fatigue.
  • Low levels can indicate anemia or other health issues.
