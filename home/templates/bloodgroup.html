Blood Group Check

Check your blood group easily with our advanced testing methods.

Step 1: Book an Appointment

Schedule your blood test at our lab or a partner facility near you.

Step 2: Visit the Lab

Come to your selected location at the scheduled time. No fasting required.

Step 3: Give Blood Sample

A quick finger prick or small blood draw from your arm (takes 5-10 minutes).

Step 4: Get Your Results

Your blood sample will be processed and you'll receive your blood group results.
