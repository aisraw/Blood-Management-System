Volunteer Opportunities

Become a Life-Saving Volunteer.Your Time Can Pump Life Into Our Community

Join our network of 2,000+ dedicated volunteers who make blood donation events possible. Whether you're assisting donors, managing logistics, or educating communities, your contribution creates a direct impact. No medical background needed - we provide full training and flexible scheduling to fit your life.

Donor Support Champions

400+

Frontline volunteers guiding donors through the process. Duties include registration assistance, post-donation care, and maintaining a positive environment. Average commitment: 4 hours/month.

Mobile Unit Crew

350+

Travel with our bloodmobiles to community events. Help set up equipment, manage inventory, and ensure safety protocols. Perfect for those who enjoy dynamic environments.

Youth Ambassadors

1.1K+

Students organizing campus blood drives and awareness campaigns. Earn community service hours while developing leadership skills.

Volunteer Requirements

  • Check Eduactional Status (Minimum SSC/HSC pass)
  • Minimum age: 18 years (17 with parental consent)
  • Commit to at least 20 hours per quarter
  • Attend mandatory orientation session
  • Pass background check (for certain roles)
  • Comfortable working with diverse populations
  • Physical ability to stand for at-least 3-hour shifts

Training will be provided for all selected volunteers.
