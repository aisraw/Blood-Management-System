Before Donating Blood

Follow these steps to ensure a smooth donation process:

  • Drink plenty of water the day before and on the day of donation.
  • Eat a healthy meal rich in iron and vitamins.
  • Avoid alcohol and caffeine 24 hours before donation.
  • Bring a valid ID and any required documents.

After Donating Blood

Take care of yourself after donating blood:

  • Rest for at least 15 minutes after donation.
  • Drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated.
  • Avoid strenuous activities for 24 hours.
  • Eat iron-rich foods to replenish your body.

Post-Donation Care Guidance

  • Rest: Take it easy for the rest of the day.
  • Hydrate: Drink plenty of water and avoid alcohol for 24 hours.
  • Eat Well: Consume iron-rich foods like spinach, red meat, and beans.
  • Avoid Heavy Lifting: Refrain from strenuous activities for at least 24 hours.
  • Monitor for Dizziness: If you feel lightheaded, lie down and rest.
