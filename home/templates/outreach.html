Mobile Blood Donation Initiatives Reaching Every Corner.

Our 25 mobile units have traveled over 5,000 miles in 2025 alone, bringing life-saving donation opportunities to corporate offices, college campuses, and rural communities. Through innovative community partnerships and awareness campaigns, we've increased first-time donors by 40% in underserved areas.

2025 Outreach Milestones

Quarterly Progress

Q1 Innovation: Launched on-site blood drives hosted at major corporations with mobile donation buses. Employee participation increased 7% through incentive programs and executive health checks.

Q2 Expansion: Established 10 new rural collection centers with solar-powered refrigeration units, now serving ~120 remote communities.

Q3 Campus Connect: University participation rate rose with semester-long donor education. Retention increased via exam-time drives.

Q4 Community Engagement: Largest donor recognition event held, honoring 450+ volunteers across 8 divisions.
